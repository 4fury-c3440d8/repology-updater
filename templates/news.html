2017-01-30

  • Enabled many more repositories. These were supported for some time, but not enabled due to limitations of web UI. These include: DragonFly BSD DPorts (these are derived from FreeBSD ports, so in the same family), older Debian (stable, testing, experimental), Ubuntu (precise, trusty, xenial, yakkety), Fedora (24, 25), Mageia (6) versions, ArchLinux AUR, CentOS 6 and 7, Rosa Linux, GoboLinux, YACP (yet another cygwin ports), GNU Guix, freshcode.club and Libregamewiki news sites, CPAN, PyPi and RubyGems upstream repositories, F-Droid and Chocolatey.
